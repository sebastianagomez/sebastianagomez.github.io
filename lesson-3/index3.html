Site Purpose

"Babyitscoldoutside" provides information about the weather depending on your locality, this way you get to learn about the weather conditions in your area before you make plans for the day, it's integrated into your google calendar to see your plans for the day.

Secondly, Our statistics provided can also be used for academic purposes or scientific purposes. This information is going to be used in school, university, or laboratory researching about different changes in that weather or climate condition over a perid of time.

The website receives feedback from metrological institutions, academic establishments and scientific organizations and enhance our website to provide the best information to any user, organization, or establishment.
